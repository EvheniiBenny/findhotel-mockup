The Hotel

Brussels City Centre, Brussels
Fabulous 8.9 10,584 reviews

The Hotel, close to the city center, is perhaps one of the most famous hotel of Brussels. The Boulevard de Waterloo forms part of the busy “Petite Ceinture” ringroad, but The Hotel is set back from it and the rooms do not face directly onto it. Close by is the Place Louise, a transport hub (Metro, trams and buses) and focal point of the smart Avenue Louise shopping area. Brussels’ historic centrepiece, the Grand Place, is a walkable 1.5km to the north, and 1.5km south is the Horta Museum, an Art Nouveau shrine.
